Privacy

Effective 27 June 2026

Overview

Timeless Objects is a static website. We do not operate user accounts, collect personal information through forms, or run analytics or advertising trackers on this catalog.

Local storage

The site may store a small preference in your browser’s session storage to avoid replaying the intro animation on every visit. This stays on your device and is not sent to us.

External links

When you follow a link to a third-party site, that site’s privacy policy applies. We do not control how other websites handle your data.

Hosting

Like most websites, basic server logs (such as IP address and browser type) may be processed by our hosting provider to deliver the site. We do not use those logs to identify visitors.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will change when we do.
